# Inquisitor Perks Documentation (Refined)

## Overview

The Inquisitor is a relentless agent of divine will, blending divine spellcasting, studied judgment, and tactical hunting prowess. Inquisitors pursue their quarry with unwavering determination, using divine magic, keen perception, and adaptable judgment abilities. This perk system provides four distinct trees, each representing a core aspect of inquisitorial power.

## Perk Trees

- **Judgment & Spellcasting** - Focus on divine judgment abilities, spell enhancement, and magical prowess
- **Hunter's Arsenal** - Focus on favored enemy bonuses, tracking, terrain mastery, and combat tactics
- **Investigation & Perception** - Focus on detection, truth-seeking, knowledge, and sensory abilities
- **Adaptable Tactics** - Focus on versatility, teamwork, battlefield control, and survival skills

## TREE 1 - Judgment & Spellcasting

### Tier 1 (4 Perks)

1. **Empowered Judgment**
   - *3 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Your judgment abilities become more potent. Increase the bonus from any judgment you activate by +1 per rank. At rank 3, you can maintain two judgments simultaneously once per encounter for 1d4 rounds.

2. **Swift Spellcaster**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Channel divine magic with practiced efficiency. Reduce the casting time of inquisitor spells by one step (standard becomes move, move becomes swift) once per encounter. Gain +2 to concentration checks.

3. **Spell Focus: Divination**
   - *2 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Your divination spells pierce through deception. Increase the DC of divination spells by +1 per rank. At rank 2, gain one additional divination spell slot per spell level you can cast.

4. **Judgment Recovery**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Your judgment powers refresh more quickly. Regain one use of your judgment ability when you reduce an enemy to 0 hit points or below. This can occur once per encounter.

### Tier 2 (4 Perks)

1. **Enhanced Bane**
   - *4 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Your weapon gains the bane property against your judged target. Add +1 damage per rank and an additional +1 to attack rolls per 2 ranks. At rank 4, your bane effect extends to all creatures of the same type as your current target.

2. **Divine Resilience**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   - Channel divine energy to bolster yourself. When you activate a judgment, gain temporary hit points equal to your inquisitor level + Wisdom modifier. These last until depleted or the judgment ends.

3. **Spell Penetration**
   - *3 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Your spells overcome magical defenses more easily. Gain +1 per rank to checks made to overcome spell resistance. At rank 3, ignore the first 5 points of an enemy's spell resistance.

4. **Persistent Judgment**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   When a judgement is toggled off the affect of the judgement is added to the player for 5 rounds, with a 20 round cooldown.

### Tier 3 (4 Perks)

1. **Greater Judgment**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Your judgment abilities reach their full potential. Choose one judgment type (destruction, healing, justice, piercing, protection, purity, resiliency, resistance, or smiting). That judgment's bonuses are doubled when you use it. You can change this selection after a long rest.

2. **Spell Metamastery**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Apply metamagic effects with minimal effort. Once per encounter, apply a metamagic feat you know to a spell without increasing its casting time or spell slot level (maximum +2 level adjustment).

3. **Righteous Strike**
   - *2 ranks, 3 points each*
   - Channel spell energy through your weapon. When you successfully cast an inquisitor spell, your next melee attack within 1 round deals an additional 2d6 damage per rank. This damage type matches your last spell cast (divine, fire, cold, etc.).

4. **Versatile Judgment**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Switch between judgments with practiced ease. You can change your active judgment as a swift action instead of declaring a new one. Additionally, you gain one additional use of judgment per day.

### Tier 4 (4 Perks)

1. **Judgment Mastery**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Achieve perfect command over your judgment abilities. You can maintain three different judgments simultaneously. Each judgment you activate lasts 2 additional rounds after you cease concentration on it.

2. **Divine Spellstrike**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Perfectly meld magic and martial prowess. Once per day, cast an inquisitor spell as a swift action and immediately make a full attack. If any attacks hit, the spell affects that target as if you had successfully used a touch attack.

3. **Inexorable Judgment**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Your judgment transcends physical limitations. Your judgment bonuses apply even against creatures normally immune to such effects. Once per day, declare an Inexorable Judgment: target must make a Will save (DC 10 + half level + Wisdom modifier) or take 1d6 damage per inquisitor level (no damage type).

4. **Supreme Spellcasting**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Your divine spellcasting reaches legendary heights. Gain one additional spell slot for each spell level you can cast. Once per day, cast any inquisitor spell you know without expending a spell slot.

## TREE 2 - Hunter's Arsenal

### Tier 1 (4 Perks)

1. **Studied Target**
   - *4 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Mark a target for focused hunting. As a move action, study a creature within 60 feet. Gain +1 per rank to attack rolls, damage rolls, and skill checks against that target. You can maintain this on one target at a time.
   - Usage: use the shared `mark` command to set or clear your quarry: `mark <target>` or `mark clear`. This command is shared with assassins and rangers and now also applies Studied Target for inquisitors (consumes a move action).

2. **Favored Terrain**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Master fighting in specific environments. Choose a favored terrain (urban, forest, underground, etc.). Gain +2 to initiative and Stealth checks in that terrain. You can change this selection weekly.

3. **Hunter's Precision**
   - *2 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Strike at vulnerable points with deadly accuracy. 5% chance per rank to reroll a damage roll and take the higher result. This applies to both weapon and spell damage.

4. **Track and Hunt**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Follow your prey with supernatural persistence. Double your Survival skill modifier when tracking.

### Tier 2 (4 Perks)

1. **Favored Enemy Enhancement**
   - *4 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Specialize in hunting specific creature types. Choose a creature type each time you take this perk (can be the same type multiple times). Gain +2 per rank to attack and damage rolls against that type. Add +1 per rank to AC against attacks from that type.

2. **Ambush Predator**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   - Strike from concealment with devastating effect. If you attack a creature that hasn't acted yet in combat or doesn't know you're there, deal an additional 3d6 damage on your first attack.

3. **Terrain Mastery**
   - *3 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Gain additional favored terrains. Choose one new favored terrain per rank. In your favored terrains, you leave no trail unless you choose to, and gain a +2 per rank bonus to Survival checks.

4. **Hunter's Endurance**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   - Your body is conditioned for long pursuits. Gain +2 move regeneration per round. 5% chance per round to remove any AFF_FATIGUE effect on the player.

### Tier 3 (4 Perks)

1. **Deadly Aim**
   - *3 ranks, 3 points each*
   - Trade accuracy for devastating power. As a swift action, take a -1 penalty to attack rolls to gain +3 per rank to damage rolls until the end of your turn. This applies to both melee and ranged attacks.

2. **Master Tracker**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Your tracking abilities become near-supernatural. You can track creatures even if they fly, teleport, or use dimension door (though the trail becomes more difficult). You automatically sense when your quarry is within 1 mile.

3. **Wilderness Stride**
   - *2 ranks, 3 points each*
   - Move through any terrain with supernatural ease. Ignore difficult terrain penalties in natural environments. At rank 2, you can move through magically created difficult terrain at half speed (or full speed if it's natural).

4. **Prey's Weakness**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Identify and exploit the weaknesses of your studied target. When you successfully identify a creature's abilities (Knowledge check), learn one damage vulnerability or weakness it possesses. Your attacks ignore half the target's damage reduction.

### Tier 4 (4 Perks)

1. **Supreme Hunter**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Become the ultimate predator. Your Studied Target bonus increases by +4. You can maintain Studied Target on up to three creatures simultaneously. When a studied target drops to 0 hit points, you can immediately study a new target as a free action.

2. **Legendary Tracker**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Track any creature anywhere. You can track creatures across planar boundaries, through water, or even through time (up to 1 week old). Once per day, you can cast Locate Creature without expending a spell slot, and the range is unlimited if the target is on the same plane.

3. **Instant Death**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Deliver a killing strike with perfect precision. Once per day, when you hit with an attack against a studied target, you can force it to make a Fortitude save (DC 10 + half level + Wisdom modifier) or be reduced to 0 hit points. On a successful save, it takes an additional 10d6 damage instead.

4. **Perfect Predator**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Achieve mastery over the hunt. All your favored enemy bonuses increase by +4. In your favored terrains, you gain tremorsense 60 feet and can see perfectly in all lighting conditions. You can automatically track any creature you've encountered before.

## TREE 3 - Investigation & Perception

### Tier 1 (4 Perks)

1. **Keen Senses**
   - *4 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Your perceptive abilities are unmatched. Gain +2 per rank to Perception checks. At rank 4, you gain darkvision 60 feet (or increase existing darkvision by 60 feet), and you can see through magical darkness as if it were dim light.

2. **Read Intentions**
   - *2 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Detect hostile intent through subtle cues. Gain +3 per rank to Sense Motive checks. At rank 2, you can determine if a creature has hostile intentions toward you or your allies as a free action.

3. **Lore Master**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Your knowledge is encyclopedic. You can use Wisdom instead of Intelligence for all Knowledge checks. Once per encounter, you can take 20 on a Knowledge check as a standard action.

4. **Detect Magic**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Perceive magical auras naturally. You can cast Detect Magic at will as a spell-like ability. You automatically detect when a spell is cast within 60 feet (though you must use Spellcraft to identify it).

### Tier 2 (4 Perks)

1. **Discern Lies**
   - *3 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Pierce through deception with divine insight. Gain +2 per rank to opposed checks against Bluff. At rank 3, you can cast Discern Lies for a number of rounds per day equal to your inquisitor level.

2. **Monster Knowledge**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   - Add your Wisdom modifier in addition to Intelligence when making Knowledge checks to identify creatures. When you successfully identify a creature, you also learn its current hit points and any conditions affecting it.

3. **Scent of Magic**
   - *2 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Detect magical auras with increasing precision. You can identify the school of magic of any magical aura you detect. At rank 2, you can sense magic items within 30 feet even without concentrating, and you automatically identify spell effects targeting you.

4. **Investigator's Eye**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   - Notice details others miss. Gain +4 to Investigation checks. When examining a scene or object, you can ask the DM three questions about it and receive truthful answers (though not necessarily complete).

### Tier 3 (4 Perks)

1. **True Seeing**
   - *2 ranks, 3 points each*
   - See through illusions and deception. At rank 1, gain +4 to saving throws against illusions, and you can see invisible creatures within 30 feet as if they were visible (though concealed). At rank 2, you can cast True Seeing once per day.

2. **Telepathic Bond**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Form mental connections with your allies. You can establish a telepathic link with allies within 100 feet. You can communicate telepathically and share what you perceive through your senses. This works even through silence effects.

3. **Aura Reading**
   - *3 ranks, 3 points each*
   - Read the spiritual essence of creatures. Detect alignment as a free action on any creature within 60 feet. At rank 2, you can also detect the creature's emotional state. At rank 3, you learn the creature's surface thoughts (Will save negates, DC 10 + half level + Wisdom).

4. **Perfect Recall**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Your memory becomes eidetic. You can perfectly recall anything you've seen or heard in the past month. Once per day, you can retroactively declare that you studied something specific you encountered, gaining information as if you had used relevant Knowledge or Investigation checks at the time.

### Tier 4 (4 Perks)

1. **Omniscient Awareness**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Your perception reaches near-divine levels. You cannot be surprised. You automatically detect any creature within 120 feet, regardless of concealment, invisibility, or barriers. You gain blindsense 60 feet and can see perfectly in all conditions.

2. **Legend Lore**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Access deep magical knowledge about anything. You can cast Legend Lore once per day. When you successfully identify a creature or object, you learn its complete history, abilities, weaknesses, and any relevant legends or stories.

3. **Mind Fortress**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Your mind becomes impenetrable. You are immune to mind-reading, thought detection, and mental intrusion. You can shield up to your Wisdom modifier allies within 30 feet from these effects. Creatures attempting to read your thoughts take 2d6 psychic damage.

4. **Universal Truth**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Truth reveals itself to you automatically. You permanently have the effects of Detect Thoughts, Discern Lies, and True Seeing active. You can ask any object or location to reveal information about recent events, and you receive accurate visions (similar to Divination spell but automatic).

## TREE 4 - Adaptable Tactics

### Tier 1 (4 Perks)

1. **Combat Versatility**
   - *3 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Adapt your fighting style to any situation. Gain +1 per rank to either attack rolls, AC, or saving throws (choose at the start of each encounter). At rank 3, you can change this choice as a swift action during combat.

2. **Teamwork Bond**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Coordinate effortlessly with allies. You count as having any teamwork feat for the purpose of granting bonuses to allies who have that feat. Gain +2 to attack rolls when flanking.

3. **Solo Tactics**
   - *2 ranks, 1 point each*
   - Fight effectively without support. When you're not adjacent to an ally, gain +1 per rank to attack rolls and AC. This bonus increases to +2 per rank if you're fighting multiple enemies alone.

4. **Tactical Movement**
   - *1 rank, 1 point*
   - Move strategically around the battlefield. You can move 5 feet as a swift action once per round. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks.

### Tier 2 (4 Perks)

1. **Adaptive Resistance**
   - *4 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Develop resistance to threats you encounter. After taking damage from an energy type (fire, cold, acid, electricity, sonic), gain resistance 5 per rank to that energy type for 1 minute. You can have resistance to up to two energy types simultaneously.

2. **Battle Awareness**
   - *1 rank, 2 points*
   - Maintain perfect awareness in combat. You can't be flanked by creatures of your level or lower. Gain +2 to initiative checks. You always act in the surprise round even if you're surprised.

3. **Cunning Warrior**
   - *2 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Use guile as well as strength. You can use Wisdom instead of Strength or Dexterity for attack and damage rolls with one weapon type of your choice per rank. This represents channeling divine insight into perfect strikes.

4. **Defensive Mastery**
   - *3 ranks, 2 points each*
   - Master defensive techniques. Gain +1 per rank to AC. At rank 3, you can add your Wisdom modifier to your AC (in addition to Dexterity) as you sense attacks before they land.

### Tier 3 (4 Perks)

1. **Battlefield Commander**
   - *2 ranks, 3 points each*
   - Direct your allies with tactical precision. As a swift action, grant one ally within 60 feet a +2 per rank bonus to their next attack roll, damage roll, or saving throw. You can use this ability your Wisdom modifier times per encounter.

2. **Survivalist**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Thrive in any environment. Gain immunity to natural environmental hazards (heat, cold, altitude, etc.). You can go twice as long without food or water. Automatically succeed on checks to avoid becoming lost.

3. **Counter-Tactics**
   - *3 ranks, 3 points each*
   - Disrupt enemy strategies. When an enemy within 30 feet uses a tactical ability (flanking, aid another, teamwork feat, etc.), you can use a reaction to impose a penalty equal to your rank on their roll. At rank 3, you can also negate the tactical benefit entirely.

4. **Resilient Fighter**
   - *1 rank, 3 points*
   - Refuse to fall in battle. Once per encounter, when you would be reduced to 0 hit points or below, you're instead reduced to 1 hit point. Additionally, gain +2 to all saving throws.

### Tier 4 (4 Perks)

1. **Master Tactician**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Achieve perfect tactical awareness. You can use Battlefield Commander as a free action and can affect up to three allies simultaneously. All allies within 60 feet of you gain a +2 bonus to initiative and can't be flanked.

2. **Legendary Resilience**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Your resilience becomes supernatural. Gain damage reduction 5/—. You're immune to ability damage, ability drain, and energy drain. Once per day, automatically succeed on a saving throw of your choice.

3. **Perfect Adaptation**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Adapt to any threat instantly. At the start of each round, choose to gain one of the following for that round: immunity to one energy type, immunity to one condition, spell resistance equal to 10 + your level, or fast healing 5.

4. **Inquisitor's Supremacy**
   - *1 rank, 4 points*
   - Achieve the pinnacle of inquisitorial power. Gain +2 to all ability scores. All your class abilities gain a +2 bonus (judgment bonuses increase, spell DCs increase, etc.). Once per day, you can take two full-round actions in the same turn.

## Perk Synergies

### Cross-Tree Combinations

**Judgment + Hunter Synergy:**
- Empowered Judgment + Studied Target: Stack judgment bonuses with studied target bonuses for devastating focus fire
- Enhanced Bane + Favored Enemy Enhancement: Maximize damage against specific creature types

**Perception + Hunter Synergy:**
- Keen Senses + Track and Hunt: Never lose a trail and spot ambushes before they happen
- Monster Knowledge + Prey's Weakness: Identify and exploit every possible weakness

**Tactics + Spellcasting Synergy:**
- Combat Versatility + Swift Spellcaster: Adapt your approach seamlessly between martial and magical
- Defensive Mastery + Divine Resilience: Create an extremely durable inquisitor

**Investigation + Tactics Synergy:**
- Battle Awareness + Omniscient Awareness: Become impossible to surprise or outmaneuver
- Aura Reading + Counter-Tactics: Read enemy intentions and disrupt them before they execute

### Single-Tree Optimization

**Judgment Tree Focus:**
- Empowered Judgment (3 ranks) → Spell Penetration (3 ranks) → Versatile Judgment → Judgment Mastery = Maximized judgment utility

**Hunter Tree Focus:**
- Studied Target (4 ranks) → Favored Enemy Enhancement (4 ranks) → Deadly Aim (3 ranks) → Supreme Hunter = Ultimate damage dealer

**Investigation Tree Focus:**
- Keen Senses (4 ranks) → Discern Lies (3 ranks) → Aura Reading (3 ranks) → Omniscient Awareness = All-seeing investigator

**Tactics Tree Focus:**
- Defensive Mastery (3 ranks) → Adaptive Resistance (4 ranks) → Resilient Fighter → Legendary Resilience = Nearly unkillable tank

## Recommended Builds

### The Divine Arbiter
*Focus: Judgment & Spellcasting with Investigation support*
- Maximize judgment versatility and spell DCs
- Use investigation perks to identify threats
- Key Perks: Empowered Judgment (3), Greater Judgment, Judgment Mastery, Keen Senses (4), Discern Lies (3)

### The Apex Predator
*Focus: Hunter's Arsenal with Tactics support*
- Specialize in hunting specific enemy types
- Maximum damage output and mobility
- Key Perks: Studied Target (4), Favored Enemy Enhancement (4), Deadly Aim (3), Supreme Hunter, Combat Versatility (3)

### The All-Seeing Eye
*Focus: Investigation & Perception with Spellcasting support*
- Perfect information gathering
- Divination specialist
- Key Perks: Keen Senses (4), Monster Knowledge, True Seeing (2), Omniscient Awareness, Spell Focus: Divination (2)

### The Versatile Inquisitor
*Focus: Balanced across all trees*
- Adaptable to any situation
- Well-rounded capabilities
- Key Perks: Empowered Judgment (2), Studied Target (2), Keen Senses (2), Combat Versatility (3), Defensive Mastery (2)

### The Team Commander
*Focus: Tactics with supporting elements*
- Enhance entire party effectiveness
- Tactical battlefield control
- Key Perks: Teamwork Bond, Battlefield Commander (2), Master Tactician, Telepathic Bond, Battle Awareness

## Notes on Incremental Perks

Several perks offer incremental benefits through multiple ranks:
- **Empowered Judgment** (3 ranks): Progressively stronger judgments, unlocks dual-judgment at rank 3
- **Studied Target** (4 ranks): Scaling bonuses to focus down priority targets
- **Favored Enemy Enhancement** (4 ranks): Can specialize deeply in one type or spread across multiple
- **Keen Senses** (4 ranks): Progresses from improved perception to supernatural senses
- **Adaptive Resistance** (4 ranks): Increases energy resistance dramatically
- **Defensive Mastery** (3 ranks): Scales from minor AC boost to Wisdom-based defense

These multi-rank perks allow for meaningful character customization and power progression throughout all tiers of play.
