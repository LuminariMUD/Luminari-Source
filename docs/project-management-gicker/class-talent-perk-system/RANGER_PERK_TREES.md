# Ranger Perk Trees - DDO-Inspired Expertise System

## Overview

The Ranger class features three distinct perk trees, each with four tiers of progression. Each tree represents a different playstyle and specialization, inspired by Dungeons & Dragons Online's enhancement system.

**The Three Trees:**
1. **Hunter** - Archery and ranged combat specialization
2. **Beast Master** - Animal companion and nature magic focus
3. **Wilderness Warrior** - Two-weapon fighting and melee combat

---

## TREE 1: HUNTER
*Master of the bow, deadly from range*

### TIER I - Cost: 1 point each

#### Archer's Focus I
- **Max Ranks:** 3
- **Description:** +1 to hit with ranged weapons per rank
- **Effect Type:** Ranged attack bonus
- **Prerequisites:** None

#### Steady Aim I
- **Max Ranks:** 3
- **Description:** +1 damage with ranged weapons per rank
- **Effect Type:** Ranged damage bonus
- **Prerequisites:** None

#### Quick Draw
- **Max Ranks:** 3
- **Description:** Reduces time to nock arrows and reload ranged weapons by 10% per rank
- **Effect Type:** Attack speed improvement with ranged weapons
- **Prerequisites:** None

#### Improved Critical: Ranged I
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** +1 critical threat range with ranged weapons (19-20 becomes 18-20)
- **Effect Type:** Critical range expansion
- **Prerequisites:** None

---

### TIER II - Cost: 2 points each

#### Archer's Focus II
- **Max Ranks:** 2
- **Description:** Additional +2 to hit with ranged weapons per rank
- **Effect Type:** Ranged attack bonus (stacks with Archer's Focus I)
- **Prerequisites:** Archer's Focus I (max rank)

#### Deadly Aim
- **Max Ranks:** 2
- **Description:** Arrows/bolts ignore 5 points of DR per rank
- **Effect Type:** DR penetration for ranged attacks
- **Prerequisites:** Steady Aim I (1 rank)

#### Manyshot
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Once per combat, fire an additional arrow at your target (does not consume additional ammunition)
- **Effect Type:** Special ranged attack ability
- **Prerequisites:** Quick Draw (2 ranks)

#### Hunter's Mark
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Mark a target for 5 rounds; +2 to hit and +1d6 damage against marked target
- **Effect Type:** Single-target debuff/damage boost
- **Prerequisites:** Archer's Focus I (1 rank)

---

### TIER III - Cost: 3 points each

#### Improved Manyshot
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Manyshot now fires 2 additional arrows and can be used twice per combat
- **Effect Type:** Enhanced special attack
- **Prerequisites:** Manyshot (1 rank)

#### Sniper
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Critical hits with ranged weapons deal +2d6 damage
- **Effect Type:** Critical damage bonus
- **Prerequisites:** Improved Critical: Ranged I

#### Longshot
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Increase effective range of ranged weapons by 50%, no penalty for long range
- **Effect Type:** Range improvement
- **Prerequisites:** Archer's Focus II (1 rank)

#### Pinpoint Accuracy
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Ignore AC bonuses from cover and concealment with ranged attacks
- **Effect Type:** Tactical advantage
- **Prerequisites:** Deadly Aim (1 rank)

---

### TIER IV - Cost: 5 points each (CAPSTONE)

#### Master Archer
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** All ranged attacks automatically critical on natural 19-20, critical multiplier increases to x4
- **Effect Type:** Massive critical enhancement
- **Prerequisites:** Sniper

#### Arrow Storm
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Once per day, unleash a devastating volley hitting all enemies in the room for 6d6 damage
- **Effect Type:** Area attack ability
- **Prerequisites:** Improved Manyshot

---

## TREE 2: BEAST MASTER
*One with nature and animal companions*

### TIER I - Cost: 1 point each

#### Enhanced Companion I
- **Max Ranks:** 3
- **Description:** Animal companion gains +5 HP and +1 AC per rank
- **Effect Type:** Companion survivability
- **Prerequisites:** None

#### Pack Tactics I
- **Max Ranks:** 3
- **Description:** You and your animal companion gain +1 to hit when flanking the same enemy per rank
- **Effect Type:** Tactical combat bonus
- **Prerequisites:** None

#### Natural Empathy I
- **Max Ranks:** 3
- **Description:** +2 bonus to all Animal Handling and Animal Empathy checks per rank
- **Effect Type:** Skill bonus
- **Prerequisites:** None

#### Spell Focus: Conjuration I
- **Max Ranks:** 2
- **Description:** +1 DC to conjuration spells (includes summon spells) per rank
- **Effect Type:** Spell DC bonus
- **Prerequisites:** None

---

### TIER II - Cost: 2 points each

#### Enhanced Companion II
- **Max Ranks:** 2
- **Description:** Animal companion gains +10 HP, +2 AC, and +2 to hit per rank
- **Effect Type:** Companion enhancement (stacks with Enhanced Companion I)
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced Companion I (max rank)

#### Feral Charge
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Animal companion's first attack each combat deals +2d6 damage
- **Effect Type:** Opening attack bonus
- **Prerequisites:** Pack Tactics I (2 ranks)

#### Nature's Remedy
- **Max Ranks:** 2
- **Description:** Healing spells cast by you are 25% more effective per rank
- **Effect Type:** Healing power increase
- **Prerequisites:** Spell Focus: Conjuration I (1 rank)

#### Shared Spells
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Beneficial spells cast on yourself also affect your animal companion
- **Effect Type:** Spell sharing
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced Companion I (2 ranks)

---

### TIER III - Cost: 3 points each

#### Alpha Bond
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Your animal companion gains +3 to all saves and immunity to fear
- **Effect Type:** Companion resilience
- **Prerequisites:** Enhanced Companion II (1 rank)

#### Coordinated Attack
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** When you and your companion attack the same target, both gain +2d4 damage
- **Effect Type:** Synergy damage bonus
- **Prerequisites:** Pack Tactics I (max rank) and Feral Charge

#### Primal Vigor
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** You and your animal companion regenerate 1 HP per round in combat
- **Effect Type:** Regeneration
- **Prerequisites:** Nature's Remedy (1 rank)

#### Greater Summons
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** All summoned creatures have +25% HP and deal +1d6 damage
- **Effect Type:** Summoning enhancement
- **Prerequisites:** Spell Focus: Conjuration I (max rank)

---

### TIER IV - Cost: 5 points each (CAPSTONE)

#### Primal Avatar
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Your animal companion becomes a Primal Beast: +50 HP, +5 AC, +5 to hit, attacks deal +3d6 damage, immune to mind-affecting
- **Effect Type:** Ultimate companion enhancement
- **Prerequisites:** Alpha Bond

#### Nature's Wrath
- **Max Ranks:** 1
- **Description:** Once per day, channel nature's fury: you and your companion gain +4 to all stats, +2d8 damage on all attacks, and fast healing 5 for 10 rounds
- **Effect Type:** Powerful transformation ability
- **Prerequisites:** Coordinated Attack and Primal Vigor

- **Tier II:** 4 perks @ 2 points each = 8-24 points
- **Tier III:** 4 perks @ 3 points each = 12-36 points
- **Tier IV:** 2 perks @ 5 points each = 10 points (capstones)

### Point Requirements
- **Full tree completion:** 32 points per tree
- **Single capstone path:** ~20-25 points (depending on prerequisites)
- **Multi-tree builds:** Viable due to tier I/II accessibility

## Design Philosophy

This perk system follows DDO's enhancement philosophy:

1. **Clear Specialization Paths:** Each tree offers a distinct playstyle
2. **Meaningful Prerequisites:** Talent tree structure with logical progression
3. **Incremental Power:** Gradual power increases through tiers
4. **Powerful Capstones:** Tier IV abilities require significant investment
5. **Internal Synergies:** Perks within each tree complement each other
6. **Build Diversity:** Multiple viable build paths and hybrid options

### Recommended Builds

#### The Sniper (Pure Hunter)
Focus on ranged damage and critical hits. Path to Master Archer for devastating ranged DPS.

#### The Beastlord (Pure Beast Master)
Maximize companion power and coordination. Path to Primal Avatar for an unstoppable animal companion.

#### The Tempest (Pure Wilderness Warrior)
Master two-weapon fighting. Path to Perfect Two-Weapon Fighting for unmatched melee attack speed.

#### The Hybrid Hunter-Warrior
Split points between Hunter and Wilderness Warrior for versatile ranged and melee capabilities.

#### The Nature's Champion
Combine Beast Master and Wilderness Warrior for a companion-assisted melee combatant.

## Implementation Notes

### Perk Categories
- `PERK_CATEGORY_HUNTER` (17)
- `PERK_CATEGORY_BEAST_MASTER` (18)
- `PERK_CATEGORY_WILDERNESS_WARRIOR` (19)

### Associated Class
- All perks: `CLASS_RANGER`

### Perk ID Range
- Suggested range: 1000-1099 (extending current ranger perk IDs)
- Current ranger perks occupy: 1000-1006

### Effect Types Required
- `PERK_EFFECT_RANGED_TOHIT` - Ranged attack bonus
- `PERK_EFFECT_RANGED_DAMAGE` - Ranged damage bonus
- `PERK_EFFECT_COMPANION_BONUS` - Animal companion enhancements
- `PERK_EFFECT_TWO_WEAPON` - Two-weapon fighting bonuses
- `PERK_EFFECT_SPECIAL` - Special abilities and unique effects
- `PERK_EFFECT_HP` - HP bonuses
- `PERK_EFFECT_AC` - AC bonuses
- `PERK_EFFECT_SKILL` - Skill bonuses

### Special Mechanics Needed
- Manyshot ability system (limited uses per combat)
- Hunter's Mark target marking system
- Animal companion stat modification system
- Proc-based ability system (Whirling Steel, Crippling Strike)
- Daily ability tracking (Arrow Storm, Nature's Wrath)
- Favored enemy bonus integration
- Two-weapon fighting penalty modification
